# Truckstop

## Owner
Drake (Adrian) — logistics and fleet operations lead.

## Operating notes
- Load board monitored daily; Drake filters for lanes matching equipment type and target RPM.
- Driver logs and HOS compliance reviewed weekly — no dispatch on loads that push hours.
- Fuel card reconciliation runs weekly against mileage reports.
- Maintenance intervals tracked per unit; Drake schedules downtime during low-volume windows.
- Broker relationships maintained by Drake; rate negotiation authority up to 10% off posted rate.

## Escalation
1. Breakdown en route → Drake coordinates roadside, notifies broker, and documents for insurance.
2. Load rejection or dispute → Drake pulls BOL, photos, and timestamps; loops in Aubrey Graham for document review.
3. Compliance audit notice → immediate hold on dispatch; Aubrey Graham pulls all required records.
